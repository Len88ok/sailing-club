Yetişkin Kursları

Information about our adult courses

Period

15-hours - devided on lessons for 3 hours

Location

Iskenderun Yelken Kulübü

Minimum age

15 years

Groups

2-3 athlets

Every year, it starts after schools close. It consists of a 15-hour training program. Adult courses are planned with trainees. Groups consist of a maximum of 3 persons. Shedule is flexible and about all changes in schedule of courses, the athlet will be notified by phone / SMS / Email beforehead.

After successful finishing all steps of learning our athlets receive certificate.

Our trainings are made on the French-made Archambault brand, Surprise model boats. The boat size is 7.69 meters.

Our adult courses for those who want to learn sailing and sea in short time.

From us

  • Equipment: boat, sailing, lifebelt, ropes
  • Classroom and studying materials
  • Changing rooms and shower
  • Interesting lessons and knowledges

From athlet

  • Spare sea shorts and T-shirt
  • Hats, Towels, Sun Cream
  • Notebook and pen
  • Whistle
  • Good mood and desire to learn
Only 599 TL

Price is setting in May for all period. You can devide the payment in two parts. In addition, every student is charged 50 TL insurance and Logbook fee for one time during course periods.

We have a good discount programm:
  • 15% for invitation of our athlet
  • 15% to our previous trainees

* Registration documents and those who do not pay can not start.

* Payments can be made by bank transfer or by credit card at the club premises prior to the course start date.

* Payment and registration will not be accepted after the beginning of the course.
